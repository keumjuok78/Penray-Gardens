About Penray Gardens

Penray Gardens are a Pick Your Own orchard that is hugely popular over the summer months for strawberries and plums. Located in Otaki just a short drive from Wellington, they are often picked out during the strawberry season so get in quick.
We are also home to the delicious “Chilli Patch” preserves which are the perfect accompaniment to any meal.
Our own Gourmet Sauces , relishes, and dressings are made on site for you. Specialty Xmas and special occasion gift boxes and baskets, Fresh fruit and vegetables, and in season P.Y.O Strawberries, Tomatoes , Chillies and Apples .
